The move comes as the Strait of Hormuz remains near-impassable due to the ongoing US-Iran standoff, and as the UAE recently negotiated a $20 billion dollar swap line with the US Treasury. *via Yahoo Finance, Reuters, BusinessDay*

**US-Iran:** Peace talks remain stalled. Trump reportedly rejected Iran's latest peace proposal while maintaining red lines on nuclear weapons. The Hormuz blockade continues to choke global energy flows. *via Yahoo Finance*

**Gold/Silver:** Gold futures tumbled more than 2% and silver fell ~3% as rising oil prices fueled inflation fears and "higher for longer" rate expectations. The precious metals selloff is counterintuitive but reflects the same dynamic seen in prior Hormuz escalations. *via Yahoo Finance*
