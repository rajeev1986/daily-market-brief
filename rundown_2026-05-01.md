## 6. Market Themes

**1. AI Capex — Winners vs. Losers Emerge**
Wednesday's Mag 7 earnings drew a sharp line: Alphabet (+10%) and Amazon (+3%) rewarded for turning AI spend into cloud revenue. Meta (−9%) and Microsoft (−2%) punished for capex hikes without clear near-term payoff. The market is no longer rewarding AI spending — only AI payoffs. *via [Forbes](https://www.forbes.com/sites/petercohan/2026/04/30/earnings-reveal-the-first-real-winners-and-losers-of-the-ai-arms-race/)*

**2. Apple Closes April as the Cleanest Mag 7 Print**
AAPL delivered a nearly perfect quarter — revenue beat, record iPhone and Services, $100B buyback, dividend raise, and a well-managed CEO transition announcement. It's the one Mag 7 name where the market got what it wanted with no capex shock. *via [CNBC](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/05/01/apple-stock-rallies-on-q2-earnings-and-q3-guidance.html)*

**3. Oil Volatility — Peace Proposal Brings Relief, But Risk Remains**
Brent hit $126 Thursday (4-year high) before pulling back to ~$108 on Iran's new peace proposal. The cycle of spike-on-escalation, drop-on-hope is repeating. Exxon's CEO warned the full market impact hasn't been felt yet. BCA Research warns Iran war likely to re-escalate later in 2026. *via [CNBC](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/05/01/exxon-ceo-iran-war-oil-strait-hormuz.html), Reuters*

**4. Sector Rotation — Energy Out, Tech Back In**
E*TRADE's monthly sector study shows Energy was the most net-sold sector (−6.63%) while Utilities (+3.21%) and Consumer Staples (+2.16%) led buying. Oil pullback today accelerates rotation back into growth/tech. *via [Morningstar](https://www.morningstar.com/news/business-wire/20260501995600/etrade-from-morgan-stanley-releases-monthly-sector-rotation-study)*

**5. Earnings Season Running Hot**
S&P 500 companies are reporting earnings 20.7% above estimates — highest surprise percentage since Q1 2021. 84%+ beat rate. The earnings backdrop is the strongest in years, providing a fundamental floor under the market even as geopolitical risk swirls. *via [FactSet](https://insight.factset.com/sp-500-earnings-season-update-may-1-2026)*
